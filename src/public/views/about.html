About Chess Insights

Learn more about our mission, our team, and the tools we've built to help you explore chess data interactively.

The Project

Chess Insights is a data-driven initiative developed during the EPFL Data Visualization course. It aims to make chess exploration accessible and intuitive through visual storytelling, interactive charts, and user-friendly interfaces.

Us

We are TeamGB — a group of passionate EPFL students focused on leveraging design, data science, and user experience to create impactful visualizations. This project is our final delivery for COM-480.

Contact

You can reach us via the GitHub repository or through the official course channels.
